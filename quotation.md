# Website Development Quotation for Your Gym

## 1. Project Overview

This document outlines the quotation for the design, development, and deployment of a custom gym management website. The proposed solution is a modern, user-friendly, and robust platform that will streamline your gym's operations, improve member management, and provide valuable insights into your business. This custom solution is tailored specifically to your needs, ensuring a perfect fit for your gym's workflow.

## 2. Feature Breakdown

### 2.1. Frontend (Web App)

The frontend is the user-facing part of the application, designed to be intuitive and efficient.

*   **Dashboard:** A comprehensive dashboard that provides a real-time overview of your gym's key metrics:
    *   Total, active, expiring, and expired members.
    *   Monthly revenue from active subscriptions.
    *   Visual charts for member distribution across different branches and plans.
    *   A list of members whose subscriptions are expiring soon.
*   **Member Management:** A powerful and easy-to-use interface to manage your members:
    *   Add new members with their details, including name, contact information, plan, and branch.
    *   View a list of all members with their subscription status.
    *   Search and filter members based on their name, branch, or plan.
    *   Edit member details and update their subscription information.
    *   Remove members from the system.
*   **Settings:** A dedicated page to configure the application settings.
*   **Responsive Design:** The website is built with a responsive design, ensuring it works flawlessly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

### 2.2. Backend (Server)

The backend is the engine of the application, handling all the business logic and data processing.

*   **Robust API:** A secure and efficient API (Application Programming Interface) that powers the frontend, enabling seamless data flow.
*   **Automated WhatsApp Notifications:**
    *   Automatic welcome messages to new members upon registration.
    *   This can be extended to send reminders for expiring subscriptions, payment confirmations, and other important announcements.
*   **Scheduled Tasks:** The system is capable of running scheduled tasks, such as daily checks for expiring memberships and sending automated reminders.

### 2.3. Database

The database is where all your valuable data is stored securely.

*   **Custom Schema:** A well-designed database schema to store all the information related to your members, plans, branches, and subscriptions.
*   **Type-Safe Database Access:** We use modern tools like Drizzle ORM to ensure that all data access is type-safe, which significantly reduces the chances of bugs and makes the system more reliable and maintainable.
